Virtual Taekwondo Korean Ambassadors cup

The 1st Virtual Taekwondo Korean Ambassadors cup was held on 18th and 19th December with the participation of more than 64 clubs around the country. Players P.D.A Madhushani and B.A.H.S. Jayasinghe of NSBM Taekwondo Club was able to win Bronze medals in Under 30 Female Poomsae Blue belt and Green belt category respectively. Special Thanks goes to the coach of the team, Captain Nalinda Rathnayaka, and Pulan Gayashan for training the students. Congratulations dear students and Thank you, for making NSBM Proud!.

Inter House Cricket Championship

The "Inter House Cricket Championship" concluded with house Citrine emerging as the Champions and Sapphire securing the Runners-up title on last Saturday, 3rd of October 2020. House Emerald gained the third place against the house Ruby after an intense battle. Prof. E.A. Weerasinghe, the Vice Chancellor, Prof. Bharatha Dodankotuwa, Head of Academics and Quality Assurance, Mr. Kasun Dissanayake, Head of Student Affairs/MIC- Cricket and along with the house leaders; Ms. Dilini Dissanayake- Citrine, Ms. Samadhi Weerathne- Ruby, Mr. Lasitha De Silva and Ms. Kalni Peiris along with Ms. Sashini Gayanika from the house Sapphire, graced the occasion. The players were awarded medals and certificates in appreciating their dedication and passion towards this global sport, Cricket.

Inter-house Soccer Tournament- NSBM Sports Fiesta 2020

The Citrine warriors lead the overall points table securing a 1-0 win over Ruby Adventures and becoming the Soccer Champions in the Inter-house Soccer Tournament held at NSBM premises today. Emerald Fighters claimed the Second Runners Up and all the teams well played with Team Spirit to compete each other. Overall, the day was full of excitement and the players of each house exhibited exceptional Sportsmanship towards victory.
